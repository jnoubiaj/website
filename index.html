FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How much funding can I qualify for?

+
Qualification amounts depend on your credit score, monthly revenue, time in business, and specific programs. Our clients typically access between $10,000 and $2,000,000+. Use the funding calculator above to get a rough estimate based on your profile.

How long does it take to get funded?

+
For credit-ready clients, credit card stacking can happen in 24–72 hours, BLOCs and term loans in 1–3 weeks. SBA loans take 30–90 days. If Foundation work is needed first, we target funding-readiness within 90–180 days.

Will applying affect my credit score?

+
We use soft pulls only during assessment and pre-qualification — your score won't be affected just by checking your options. Hard inquiries only happen at final application, and we time and batch those strategically.

What is the minimum credit score required?

+
We work with all credit levels. Below 580 → Foundation program first. 580–699 → BLOCs, term loans, and entry products. 700+ → credit stacking unlocked. 740+ → premium programs including SBA loans.

How is CapitalQuest different from a regular lender or broker?

+
We're not a lender and not just a broker. We're a credit optimization and funding strategy firm — we actively improve your financial profile before and during the funding process. No bank or marketplace does that.

Is there a cost or obligation to get started?

+
Your initial consultation is free with no obligation. We'll assess your profile and recommend a path — honestly. Fees for programs are explained clearly before you commit to anything.
