FAQ

Credit Repair Questions Answered

How long does credit repair take?

+
Most clients see meaningful score improvement within 60–90 days. Significant results — removing multiple collections or late payments — typically take 90–180 days. We provide monthly updates so you're never in the dark about where your score stands.

Can you guarantee a specific score increase?

+
No reputable credit repair firm can legally guarantee specific results, and we won't pretend to. What we can guarantee is that we'll execute every legitimate strategy available and report your progress honestly each month. Our average client sees 40–80 point improvements within 90 days.

Is credit repair legal?

+
Absolutely. The Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) gives every consumer the legal right to dispute inaccurate, outdated, or unverifiable information on their credit report. We operate entirely within federal law — we never use illegal tactics like file segregation or creating a new credit identity.

What if the negative items are accurate?

+
Accurate negative items can still sometimes be removed. We pursue goodwill deletion requests for late payments, and pay-for-delete negotiations for collections. These don't always work, but we pursue every legitimate avenue. Even when items stay, we optimize the rest of your profile to minimize their impact.
