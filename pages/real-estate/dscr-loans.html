What We Do

Qualify on the Property's Income — Not Yours

DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio. It measures whether the property's rental income covers the mortgage payment. If the rent covers the debt, you qualify — regardless of your personal income, employment status, or how many properties you already own.

DSCR loans are 30-year investment mortgages built for real estate investors who can't — or don't want to — document personal income the conventional way. No tax returns, no W-2s, no DTI calculations.

  • Loan amounts up to $5,000,000
  • Up to 85% LTV on purchase; 75–80% on cash-out refi
  • 30-year fixed or ARM terms available
  • No income verification, no tax returns, no W-2s
  • No limit on number of financed properties
  • Qualify on rental income, not personal DTI
  • Short-term rental income (Airbnb/VRBO) accepted by select lenders

FAQ

DSCR Loan Questions

What does DSCR mean and how is it calculated?

+
DSCR stands for Debt Service Coverage Ratio. It measures whether the property's rental income covers the mortgage payment. The formula is: Monthly Rent ÷ Monthly Mortgage Payment = DSCR. A DSCR of 1.0 means the rent exactly covers the mortgage. A DSCR of 1.25 means the property earns 25% more than the payment — which is a strong profile.

Do I need to show my personal income or tax returns?

+
No. That is the biggest advantage of DSCR loans. Qualification is based on the property's rental income — not your personal income, W-2s, tax returns, or debt-to-income ratio. Self-employed investors and business owners with complex financials qualify without the usual documentation burden.

How many DSCR loans can I have at once?

+
There is no standard limit. Many of our lender programs allow investors to hold multiple DSCR loans simultaneously — this is one of the primary advantages over conventional mortgages, which cap at 10 financed properties. DSCR loans are designed for portfolio investors who scale.

Can I use a DSCR loan for short-term rentals like Airbnb?

+
Some lenders accept short-term rental income for DSCR calculations. Qualification may require documented rental history (12 months of Airbnb income) or a market rent analysis from an appraiser. Not all lenders in our network allow this — we'll identify the right lender for your property type.

Can I do a cash-out refinance with a DSCR loan?

+
Yes. Cash-out refinances are available up to 75–80% LTV with no income verification. This is one of the most powerful tools for portfolio growth — pull equity from existing rentals and use it as a down payment on the next property, all without documenting personal income.
